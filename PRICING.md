## The Credit System

Credits are the unit of work. Each credit represents a done-for-you AI-powered deliverable — researched, built, tested, and handed off by Mike. This isn't a SaaS tool where you click buttons. You're buying Mike's expertise applied through AI tooling to your actual business.

**1 credit = $375 of done-for-you AI services**

That rate is consistent across all plans. Higher plans don't cost more per credit — they mean more gets done each month.

Services have a credit cost based on the value and work involved. A quick automation might cost 1 credit. A full lead sourcing system might cost 4. There are no fixed "tiers" — the credit cost reflects the actual scope.

> **Why no bulk discount on credits?** The value of the work doesn't change with scale — what changes is how much gets done each month. Clean, consistent credit pricing means clients always know what they're getting.

> **Why no mid-plan bonus credits?** Adding bonus credits is just a discount with extra steps. Annual pricing is the one structured discount: clients commit for a year and get two months free. That's it.

## Onboarding

**All new clients pay a one-time onboarding fee: $500**

This covers:
- Discovery call and business audit
- 90-day build roadmap
- Website mockup (included — Mike leads with this in outreach anyway)
- New website build and hosting setup on DesignSpore's Hetzner infrastructure

**Website rebuild is included for all new clients.** Every client gets their site rebuilt and hosted by Mike on his Hetzner server. This isn't optional — it's how Mike can deploy code changes, integrate AI tools cleanly, and maintain the systems he builds without fighting with existing site setups. The mockup is usually shown during the sales process; the full build uses the client's first month's credits or is folded into onboarding depending on scope.

## Why This Pricing Is Right

*(This section should be adapted and used on the AI Services sales page and on individual module detail pages. See: AI Services Page Rebuild task.)*

### vs. Hiring In-House
A competent AI/automation specialist costs $80,000–$120,000/year ($6,700–$10,000/mo) — before payroll taxes, benefits, or the ramp-up period where they're still learning your business. And they still won't have Mike's 10-year design and brand perspective.

### vs. Traditional Agencies
Agency retainers for comparable output: $5,000–$15,000/mo. They have overhead, account managers, and junior staff doing the actual work. You're paying for the org chart. Here, you're paying for the person doing the work.

### vs. DIY Tools
Tools like GoHighLevel, HubSpot, Zapier — you pay the subscription AND still need someone to set them up AND maintain them. The setup cost alone often exceeds a month of DesignSpore. And those tools don't think strategically about your business.

### The Real Comparison
$1,500/mo for a working AI lead system running 24/7 vs. $1,500 in ad spend that generates 2–3 leads this month and nothing next month. The system compounds. The ads stop when you stop paying.

## Credit Rollover Policy

Credits do **not** roll over. Each month is scoped to that month's builds. This keeps the work focused and prevents a backlog that erodes quality.

Exception: if Mike cannot begin an activation within the billing period due to scheduling, the credit carries forward.
