04The Gap

Why existing models fall short

No existing model combines all the elements in a repeatable way.

Resorts — seasonal comfort and services, but built for short, passive stays.

Retreats & festivals — powerful experiences, but not a long-term lifestyle.

RV parks, glamping & campsites — nature and basic amenities, but no cultural continuity.

Co-living / co-working — shared infrastructure and community, but urban, transient, not for families.

Wellness membership clubs — health amenities, but visit-based, not lifestyle-integrated.

Intentional communities / ecovillages — holistic vision, but no professional operations, capital discipline, or repeatability.

Portal.Place is designed to combine them all.

A new model

Portal.Place combines real estate, membership, education & technology.

A membership-based village campus, built to scale into a network:

  • Tech-enabled operations & repeatable standards — The Village Stack

A repeatable operating model for lifestyle-first living in the post-AI world.

09Business Model

Four revenue layers

01
Founding Membership (Timeshare-like)

3 tiers, $50K–$100K one-time, capped at 50 — equity + lifetime access; funds cabin buildout.

Annual Membership (Subscription)

$200–$350/yr recurring revenue that anchors demand and powers network effects.

Operating Revenue (Growth)

Nightly stays & rentals (RV, tenting, cabins, dome, gazebo); events (weddings, reunions, tournaments); services (golf, sauna, laundry, shuttle).

04
Network Expansion (Future)

Village AI dashboard; templates deployed to partner sites; licensing, partnerships & consulting.

Multiple scalable upside layers on top of proven operating revenue.
