14How It Works

Simple, flexible, yours.

Founding Member credits

Your season entitlement sits in your member account as credits. Log in, see your balance, and apply it to cabin nights, RV nights, the gazebo, or recreation — however your summer takes shape.

Priority windows by tier

Founders book before the public: Cabin Max 90 days out, Cabin & RV 60 days out, then the public. Peak dates carry fair-use limits so every member gets a real shot at the best times.

A graceful exit

If you ever wish to step away, the company has the first right to repurchase your membership. It may also pass to an immediate family member — so your place can stay in the family.

A stake, not a deed

Your equity is a non-voting share in the membership company — not a deed, lot, or piece of land. No governance obligations, but a real voice, worked through together at the Gathering.
