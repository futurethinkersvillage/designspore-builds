02The Opportunity

The most fundable asset on the land

We already own and operate a 400-acre resort in BC, Canada — water, power, roads, staff, and guests are in place.

Cabins are the single most financeable unit on that land: quick to build, winter-enabled so they earn year-round, and each raises 6–7× its build cost in membership capacity.

This fund lets you finance that build — and share in what the cabins earn until you’re repaid.
