02The Opening

Nobody owns creative CNC

Generic CNC clips already run as b-roll under a million unrelated videos — the appetite for the machine is proven. Yet no brand has claimed the creative side of it.

What fills the feed today

Practical, repeatable builds — signs, racks, reliefs, one more walnut cutting board. Genuinely useful. Endlessly the same.

Useful · repetitive · forgettable

What’s missing

Cinematic, ambitious, genuinely creative fabrication — the kind of piece people stop scrolling for, and can’t help sharing.

Creative · cinematic · unforgettable
