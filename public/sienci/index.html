02The Opening

Nobody owns creative CNC

Generic CNC footage is everywhere — used as b-roll under unrelated videos. People clearly think it’s cool. Yet no brand is dominating the creative niche. That’s the gap.

What’s out there

A guy on camera taking 30 minutes to explain a wine rack, clipart signage, an ashtray, a family-photo relief. Cabinets. Spoons.

Practical · repetitive · forgettable

What’s missing

High-quality, cinematic, entertaining footage of highly creative and technically challenging CNC projects. Weird enough to go viral.

Creative · cinematic · unforgettable

Leave the cabinet videos to Shopsaber. Sienci is the accessible on-ramp for creatives — own that space.
