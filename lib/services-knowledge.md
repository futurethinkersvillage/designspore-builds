## Core Services

### 1. AI Chatbot Setup
**What it does:** A custom-trained chatbot installed on your website that answers questions 24/7, qualifies leads, and hands off warm prospects to you.

**Problem it solves:** Customers visit your site at 11pm, have a question, and leave when nobody answers. A chatbot captures that lead before they find a competitor.

**What's included:**
- Custom chatbot trained on your services, FAQs, and policies
- Installed on your website (works with any platform)
- Escalation logic: knows when to say "let me connect you with the team"
- Monthly monitoring and updates

**Typical business outcome:** 20–40% more leads captured from existing website traffic.

---

### 2. Missed-Call Text-Back
**What it does:** Automatically texts anyone who calls your business and doesn't get through. Sends within seconds: "Hey, sorry we missed you! We'll call back shortly — or text us here."

**Problem it solves:** 67% of callers who can't reach a business on the first try don't call back. They go to a competitor.

**What's included:**
- Automated SMS response system integrated with your phone number
- Customizable message templates
- Conversation thread so you can pick up by text if you're busy

**Typical business outcome:** Recover 15–25% of leads that would have been lost to voicemail.

---

### 3. Review Automation
**What it does:** Automatically sends review requests to customers after a job is completed. Directs happy customers to Google, catches unhappy ones before they post publicly.

**Problem it solves:** Most satisfied customers don't leave reviews without being asked. Unhappy ones do. This flips the ratio.

**What's included:**
- Automated SMS or email after job completion
- Smart routing: positive sentiment → Google review link; negative → private feedback form
- Monthly reporting on review velocity and rating trends

**Typical business outcome:** 3–5x more Google reviews per month; consistent 4.8+ rating.

---

### 4. Estimate Follow-Up Automation
**What it does:** Automatically follows up with leads who received a quote but haven't responded. Sends timed reminders over 5–10 days so no warm lead goes cold.

**Problem it solves:** Most businesses send a quote and follow up once (or never). Automated follow-up closes 20–35% more estimates without any extra effort.

**What's included:**
- Automated SMS and/or email sequence after quote is sent
- Stops automatically when the lead responds or books
- Customizable timing and messaging

---

### 5. Lead Response Automation
**What it does:** Instantly responds to new inbound leads (from your website, ads, or referrals) with a personalized message and qualification questions.

**Problem it solves:** The odds of qualifying a lead drop by 80% if you wait more than 5 minutes to respond. Most businesses respond in hours or days.

**What's included:**
- Instant automated response on lead submission
- Qualification questions to understand the lead's needs
- Notification to you with qualified lead summary

---

### 6. CRM Cleanup & Integration
**What it does:** Connects your existing tools (CRM, scheduling software, forms) into a single automated workflow so nothing falls through the cracks.

**Problem it solves:** Data scattered across spreadsheets, inboxes, and apps leads to missed follow-ups and lost revenue.

### 7. FAQ Automation
**What it does:** Builds an automated FAQ system for common questions — by SMS, chatbot, or email. Customers get answers instantly; your team spends less time on the phone.

### 8. Custom Website (AI-Enhanced)
**What it does:** A professionally designed website built with AI-powered features baked in from day one (chatbot, lead capture, review automation). Not a template — custom-built for your business.

**Problem it solves:** Most small business websites are digital brochures with no lead capture. A Design Spore site is a lead-generation machine.
